<canvas-video>

A Web Component that plays video or your webcam and blits it to a canvas in a number of ways. Also gives getter/setter access to the canvas context, as well as events out frame data as it refreshes.

  • Simple Playback

    Just play a video or use your webcam. If this all you want to do, you may as well use the video tag

    camera demo / video demo

  • Simple Playback (covermode)

    play a video, fill and crop to allotted space (no letterboxing)

    camera demo / video demo

  • Render to internal canvas

    Here we're watching not the actual camera/video feed but the canvas. We've set the refresh interval to 250ms to make it super obvious

    camera demo / video demo

  • Scale Canvas

    Use the internal canvas for display, but also scale. Kinda looks bad and useless, but scaling the canvas could be a useful feature elsewhere

    camera demo / video demo

  • Scale and Copy to Canvas

    Use the frame data event to trigger drawing to another canvas, scaling the output

    camera demo / video demo

  • Steal Internal Canvas

    Instead of multiple canvas drawing, steal the video player's internal canvas context for ourselves outside of the component. Be sure you aren't using "useCanvasForDisplay" or the video element will be hidden

    camera demo / video demo
